Volkihar Castle

Volkihar Castle is the ancient seat of the Volkihar vampire clan, perched on the edge of the Sea of Ghosts. The castle is shrouded in darkness and mystery, serving as the center of power and intrigue for the vampire lineage. It is heavily guarded by powerful vampires and is home to numerous dark secrets, including the Soul Cairn entrance hidden within its depths.

Serana, daughter of Lord Harkon, resides in the castle and plays a pivotal role in Skyrim's vampire conflicts. The castle's architecture combines imposing stone fortifications with eerie, gothic interiors reflecting centuries of vampiric rule. Explorers of the castle may encounter enchanted rooms, secret passages, and a treasury of ancient vampire artifacts.

Volkihar Castle - The imposing home of the Volkihar vampire clan.
Entrance to the Soul Cairn - Hidden within the castle.
Serana - Daughter of Lord Harkon and key character of the Dawnguard storyline.
Vampire - Guarding the castle and its secrets.
