The Blades

The Blades are an ancient order sworn to protect the Dragonborn and the rulers of Tamriel. Once elite agents of the Septim Dynasty and the Empire, they have fallen from glory after the destruction of the Imperial City and relentless persecution by the Thalmor.

In Skyrim, only two Blades have survived in hiding: Delphine, the last Grandmaster, and Esbern, a legendary loremaster. Through them, the Dragonborn learns the truth about Alduin’s return and the ancient prophecy describing the World-Eater’s rise.

Blades believe their sacred duty is the eradication of dragons. Their renewed headquarters, Sky Haven Temple, becomes a strategic base for recruiting new members and confronting Alduin’s followers. However, their ideology often conflicts with that of the Greybeards, especially regarding the fate of Paarthurnax.

Delphine — last surviving Grandmaster of the Blades.
Esbern — a legendary loremaster and dragon historian.
Alduin — the World-Eater, the great nemesis of the Blades.
