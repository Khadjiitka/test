Psijic Order

The Psijic Order is the oldest monastic society dedicated to the study of magic and the preservation of mystical knowledge. Originating from the Isle of Artaeum, they remain neutral in political conflicts and act only when the balance of the world is threatened.

During the events in Skyrim, the Psijics intervene due to the discovery of the Eye of Magnus — a relic of unimaginable power capable of altering reality. Their representative, Quaranir, guides the Dragonborn to prevent disaster and restore equilibrium.

The Psijics move through time and space with ease, appearing and vanishing instantaneously. Their power is subtle, unmatched, and often feared by other magical orders.

A Psijic monk — master of ancient and forbidden magic.
