Stormcloaks

The Stormcloaks are a rebel faction led by **Ulfric Stormcloak**, Jarl of Windhelm. They fight against the Empire, claiming that Skyrim must be free from Imperial control and Thalmor influence. Their struggle is not just political — to them, it is spiritual, tied to the worship of Talos, banned by the Empire after the Great War.

Ulfric is a controversial figure: • To some, he is a hero fighting for Nord traditions. • To others, a warmonger exploiting patriotism for personal power. His right hand, **Galmor Stone-Fist**, commands the Stormcloak armies across the holds and is known for uncompromising loyalty and fierce determination.

The Stormcloaks believe that Skyrim should choose its own High King, reject the White-Gold Concordat, and defend its honor against the Aldmeri Dominion. Their blue-colored cloaks and bear-pelt armor symbolize ancient Nordic heritage and pride.
