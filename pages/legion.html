Imperial Legion

The Imperial Legion represents the military arm of the Empire, enforcing imperial law and order across all of Tamriel. In Skyrim, its presence is deeply tied to the ongoing civil war, where the Legion battles the Stormcloaks to maintain control and preserve the unity of the Empire.

The Legion in Skyrim is commanded by **General Tullius**, a stern and practical strategist sent from Cyrodiil to crush the rebellion. He is supported by **Legate Rikke**, a dedicated Nord who believes that Skyrim must remain part of the Empire to stand strong against the Aldmeri Dominion. Rikke plays a crucial role as the bridge between Nords and Imperials, often negotiating with hold leaders and guiding the Dragonborn through Legion operations.

The Legion's goal is to ensure peace, stability, and resistance against the Thalmor—who seek to dominate Tamriel from behind the scenes. Many Imperial soldiers believe that victory in Skyrim is the only way to prevent a second Great War.

Imperial Armor — worn by Legion soldiers and officers.
